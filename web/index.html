About this map

This app interpolates point-based nitrate concentrations from well water samples and evaluates their association with census tract level cancer incidence rates using regression analysis. The user selects distance decay exponent (k) to control how quickly the influence of well nitrate measurements decreases with distance when interpolating the nitrate surface.

Front End: MapLibre GL JS , Vite, Vanilla JS

Back End: Flask, Geopandas, Numpy, Scipy, Rasterio, Statsmodels, Shapely, Pyproj
